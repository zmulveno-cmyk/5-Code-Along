About Copper City Makerspace

Copper City Makerspace is a community-driven workshop where people of all skill levels come together to learn, prototype, and create using shared tools and expertise.

Our Mission

We provide affordable access to fabrication tools, mentorship, and project-based learning to empower creative problem solving and local innovation.

Who We Serve

Students, hobbyists, small business owners, and curious community members — everyone is welcome to explore hands-on making and digital fabrication.

Community Impact

Through youth programs, public workshops, and partnerships with local schools, we build practical skills and support neighborhood entrepreneurship.
