About Copper City Makerspace

Copper City Makerspace is a community-driven workshop where people of all skill levels come together to learn, prototype, and create using shared tools and expertise.

Our Mission

We provide affordable access to fabrication tools, mentorship, and project-based learning to empower creative problem solving and local innovation.

Who We Serve

Students, hobbyists, small business owners, and curious community members — everyone is welcome to explore hands-on making and digital fabrication.

Community Impact

Through youth programs, public workshops, and partnerships with local schools, we build practical skills and support neighborhood entrepreneurship.

Workshops & Tools

From woodworking and metalwork to electronics and 3D printing, our space offers a rotating schedule of instructor-led classes and open lab hours.

Digital Fabrication

Access to 3D printers, laser cutters, and CNC routers — classes teach file prep, safety, and workflow from concept to finished part.

Electronics & Coding

Intro to microcontrollers, soldering clinics, and interactive projects that combine hardware and software for makers of all ages.

Wood & Metal Shops

Tool training for saws, drills, and metalworking equipment — supervised open shop times help you build durable projects safely.

Membership & Visit

We welcome drop-ins for tours and offer tiered memberships for frequent users. Members receive access to advanced equipment and discounted classes.

Getting Started

Sign up for an orientation to learn shop rules, safety procedures, and how to reserve equipment — orientations run weekly.

Hours & Location

Open Tue-Sat, 10:00 AM-8:00 PM. Located at 88 Forge Lane, downtown — free street parking after 6 PM and bike racks at the front door.

Volunteer & Teach

Interested in teaching a class or volunteering? We partner with experienced makers to expand offerings and mentor new members.

Upcoming Event

Community build night — bring a small project and collaborate with members. Free for members, $10 suggested donation for guests.
